    Welcome

    The Intelligent Systems Group (ISG) is a research group devoted to the research of Artificial Intelligence and its applications. ISG is composed by researchers associated to different departments (Computer Engineering, Computer Science and Mathematics) of the Unviersity of Alcalá, in Spain.

    Our main research topics involve space robotics, Machine Learning, UAV/UGV cooperation, planning & scheduling and bioinspired algorithms. The main application domains are telecare systems, sensor networks and manufacturing.
